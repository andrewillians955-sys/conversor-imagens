Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information of users who access and use our WebP and HEIC to JPG and PNG image converter. Our commitment is to provide a secure, transparent experience that complies with applicable laws, including the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD).

Processing of images and uploaded files

The images you select or drag into the converter are processed exclusively in your browser using client-side technology. We do not upload these files to our servers and we do not store image content. All processing happens locally on your device, ensuring maximum privacy.

Browsing data and cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to collect aggregated and anonymized browsing information, such as pages visited, time on site, and device type. This data is primarily used to:

  • Improve site performance and usability;
  • Measure access and engagement statistics;
  • Display relevant ads through partners such as Google AdSense.

Google DART cookies and personalized ads

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's previous visits to this and other websites on the internet. In particular, Google uses the DART cookie to show ads to users based on their visits to this site and/or other sites.

Google's use of the DART cookie enables Google and its partners to serve ads to users based on visits to this site and/or other sites on the internet. Users can opt out of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ads policy page or by adjusting the ad settings in their browser.

Other advertising partners may also use cookies and similar identifiers to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can manage your preferences through our cookie consent banner, which lets you accept all cookies or reject non-essential ones.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personally identifiable data with third parties, except when necessary to:

  • Comply with legal or regulatory obligations, or lawful requests from competent authorities;
  • Protect our rights, property, and safety, as well as the rights, property, and safety of our users;
  • Enable the operation of strictly necessary third-party services, such as ad providers.

Data subject rights

Under the LGPD, you have several rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Confirmation of processing and access to any personal data collected;
  • Correction of incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data;
  • Anonymization, blocking, or deletion of unnecessary, excessive, or non-compliant data;
  • Data portability to another service provider, when applicable;
  • Information about data sharing with third parties;
  • Revocation of consent when processing is based on that legal ground.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights or have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contato@convertflash.com.

Policy updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect legal, regulatory, or functional changes to the site. We recommend reviewing this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.
