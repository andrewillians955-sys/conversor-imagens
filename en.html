Frequently asked questions

What is WebP and why do I need to convert it to JPG?

WebP is an image format created by Google that offers strong compression. Many websites and devices still prefer or require JPG for compatibility with printers, editors, and social networks. Converting to JPG helps ensure your image works everywhere.

What is HEIC and why does it appear on my iPhone?

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is the default photo format used by iPhones and iPads starting with iOS 11. It takes up less space than JPG, but not every app or website supports HEIC. Converting to JPG lets you use your photos on any device or service.

Is the conversion really secure? Are my files uploaded to the internet?

Yes, it is secure. In this tool, all processing happens in your own browser. Your images never leave your computer or phone, so there is no upload to a server and no cloud storage. The JPG file is also created locally, and only you decide whether to download it.
