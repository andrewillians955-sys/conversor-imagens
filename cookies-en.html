What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognize your device, remember some of your preferences, and improve your browsing experience.

Types of cookies we use

Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic operation of the site, such as:

  • Remembering whether you accepted or rejected non-essential cookies;
  • Ensuring pages load correctly and securely;
  • Keeping interface and language settings.

Without these cookies, the site may not function as expected. For that reason, they cannot be disabled by our systems, although you may block them directly in your browser, which may affect some features.

Advertising and AdSense cookies

We use third-party cookies, such as Google AdSense, to display relevant ads and measure campaign performance. These cookies may:

  • Record which ads were shown and clicked;
  • Prevent the same ad from being shown repeatedly;
  • Allow personalized ads based on your interests.

You can choose to reject non-essential cookies through our consent banner. In that case, you may still see ads, but they may be less relevant to your profile.

How to manage and clear cookies in your browser

Most browsers let you manage cookies in detail. In general, you can:

  • Block all cookies;
  • Block third-party cookies;
  • Delete cookies already stored;
  • Set specific permissions for selected websites.

Check your browser's help pages to learn how:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data;
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data;
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions;
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy.

By continuing to use our site after setting your cookie preferences, you agree to the terms of this Cookie Policy.
