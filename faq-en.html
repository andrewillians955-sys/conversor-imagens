Questions about conversion and security

Are my images sent to any server?

No. All WebP and HEIC image conversion to JPG or PNG happens directly in your browser using local, client-side resources. The files are never sent to our servers and are not stored by us.

Do you keep a copy of the converted images?

We do not store copies of the original images or the converted images. The process happens only in your device's memory, and the final file is made available for download under your full control.

Is the site really free to use?

Yes. Our image converter is completely free for personal use. We keep the project running through ads served by partners such as Google AdSense, always in compliance with the platform's privacy and content policies.

Is the site compliant with the LGPD?

Yes. We minimize personal data collection and only process anonymized browsing information when needed for statistical and advertising purposes. Images submitted for conversion are not collected or identified, which significantly reduces the risk of exposing sensitive data.

How do you use cookies and browsing data?

We use essential cookies for the site's operation and third-party cookies, such as Google AdSense, for ad personalization and performance measurement. You can accept or reject non-essential cookies through our consent banner, and also manage them directly in your browser settings.
